Why Computers are Stupid

Computers are stupid because they interpret instructions literally. This makes them very unforgiving since a small mistake by a programmer can cause huge problems in a program. Computer is honest. It does what you tell it to do. Therefore it is better to be clear on the instruction so you truly understand what you want it to do.

Inline vs Block Elements

HTML elements are either inline or block. Block elements form an "invisible box" around the content inside of them.
